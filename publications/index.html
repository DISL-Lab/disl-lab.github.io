Publications

We publish our research in high-impact conferences and journals within the field of Computer Science. We have collaborated with top IT tech companies such as AWS AI Labs, META, and NAVER AI Lab.

As of now, we have published more than 60+ papers in CS top conferences across various domains, including NLP (ACL, EMNLP, NAACL), CV (ICCV, CVPR), ML (NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, AAAI), DM (KDD, CIKM, ICDM, WWW, SIGMOD).
